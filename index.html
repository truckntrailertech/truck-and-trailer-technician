About Truck & Trailer Technician

Founded in 2023 and serving the Hamilton and Brantford areas, Truck and Trailer Technician is dedicated to providing the highest standard of mobile heavy-duty repair.

Our mission is to minimize your downtime by bringing expert mechanical solutions directly to your yard or the roadside. Whether you manage a large fleet of dry vans and flatbeds or are a dedicated owner-operator with a Peterbilt or Kenworth, we combine advanced diagnostics, structural welding expertise, and strict MTO compliance to keep your business moving forward. Reliability is our core value, and 24/7 support is our promise.
